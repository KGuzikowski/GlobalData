Description
Instantly cover unwanted grays and roots or fill in thinning areas for fuller-looking hair. Our talc-free, water & sweat resistant formula clings to hair strands to absorb excess oil and give you flawless-looking locks that last all day.
How To Use
Apply to dry hair using the small end of the brush, starting from the scalp and working your way out. Press powder firmly onto areas where hair is thinning, graying or needs a color touch-up. Apply more as needed, gently removing any excess with the large side of the brush.
