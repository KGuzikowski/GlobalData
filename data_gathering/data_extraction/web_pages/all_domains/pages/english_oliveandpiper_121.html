DESCRIPTION

A delicate gold bracelet adorned with a trio of gems. This simple style is just what you need to add a touch of luxe to your everyday look.   

Shown here with the Meja Pendant and Demi Studs

✨  Vermeil Collection

This demi-fine jewelry collection features styles crafted with a thick layer of 18K gold on sterling silver, and set with Moissanite, diamond-like stones. 18K gold has a warmer, golden-yellow hue compared to 14K. 

DETAILS

Vermeil: A thick layer of 18K solid gold on sterling silver. 

18K gold has a warmer, golden-yellow hue compared to 14K gold. 

Moissanite: These lab-grown gems are remarkably similar to diamonds. They’re almost as durable, have more brilliance and perhaps a little more sparkle too! 

  • 6" length + 1" extension
  • 0.03 oz
  • Vermeil - 18K solid gold on sterling silver
  • Lobster clasp closure
  • Moissanite stones
  • Hypoallergenic, nickel free
